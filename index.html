About Us

Tucked away in the corner of Wellington, Grand Cafe is a local neighbourhood cafe and bar. Latte is all about doing what we love, in a city that we love. We keep stocked with local produce and pastries. Bring your family for a bite, or meet your friends for a coffee or warm drink.
